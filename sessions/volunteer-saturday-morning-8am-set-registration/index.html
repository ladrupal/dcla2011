We need your help throughout the day during the camp. Please considering volunteering for one of the below. Go to the Registration Area “Volunteer Table” for signing up, and instructions. There are other tasks available, and more info will be posted, and available at the Volunteer Table.

Registration Area
– Handing out Badges, with goodie bag

Volunteer Table
– Assist an Attendee who wants to volunteer find a task for them.
— Either in current hour, next session, or later.
— Tell them where to go, and who to contact for instructions (if any)

Assist with A/V, Camtasia, etc, in each presentation Room.

If you can also bring a case of water (0.5l bottles) to camp, add a comment and let us know (we’re looking for five cases/day). Save your receipt and we’ll reimburse you.
