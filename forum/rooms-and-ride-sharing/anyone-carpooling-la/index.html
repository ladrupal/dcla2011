The train should work if you

Submitted by wizonesolutions on Tue, 2011-08-02 11:23.

The train should work if you can’t find a ride. I was looking into this a lot before. It’s a pain, though. You have to go to Union Station and take the Metrolink Orange County line, I believe…which does go to Irvine. But I remember there was something kinda tricky about it. Try Google Maps, and once you get an idea of it, re-search on metro.net.

The Amtrak Pacific Surfliner goes through Irvine as well, though probably costs more than Metrolink.

From what I saw, public transportation is a big pain from Irvine Metrolink to UCI (bad enough that I made sure I’d have a car for DCLA)…so don’t forget to hit me up if you need to be picked up from the station.
