DrupalCamp LA 2011

Eat+Drink

Note: this is an archived site. Visit us at http://ladrupal.org.

Breakfast

You're on your own for breakfast, but we'll probably have some coffee brewing, starting at 8am.

Lunch

Directly across Campus Dr. (a 5 minute walk) is the University Center shopping center with a variety of sit-down and fast food restaurants. Directions: exit the conference center at the doors near the parking structure and head right along Pereira Dr until you reach the rose garden (about 500 ft). Then go left and take the bridge over Campus Dr. to the University Center.


View DrupalCamp LA 2011 @ UCI in a larger map

  • Steelhead brewery
  • Veggie Grill
  • Asia Noodle Cafe
  • Chakra Indian Cuisine
  • Lees Sandwiches
  • Z Pizza
  • Peet's Coffee (hours: 6a-11p)
  • Jack-in-the-box
  • In-n-Out Burger
  • KFC/Taco Bell

A little farther down Campus Dr. (a 15 minute walk) is Campus Plaza with:

  • Del Taco
  • Gina's Pizza
  • Starbucks Coffee

A 5-minute drive off-campus at Bison Ave and Bayswater is The Bluffs, a small shopping center with:

  • Panera Bread Bakery (free WiFi; hours: 7a-8p)
  • Islands
  • Carl's Jr.
  • Chipotle
  • Quiznos
  • Daphne's Greek Cafe
  • Pasta Bravo
  • Pei Wei

All on-campus retail restaurants are closed on the weekend with the exception of Stabucks and Wendys, upstairs in the Student Center open on Saturday (only) 10:00am-4:00pm.

Drupal after Dark, Saturday, August 6, 7PM

UC Irvine Anthill Pub

We have the UC Irvine Anthill Pub reserved for Saturday night.

The Pub will have their kitchen open for dinner so you will be able to purchase something to eat while hanging out, cash only. There are ATMs nearby.

After hours / nightlife

Check the forums for after hours activities. For those interested in seeing a bit of the night life in Orange County we recommend checking out the following scenes:

  • Irvine Spectrum (Irvine @ 405 & 5 freeways)
  • Fashion Island (Newport Beach off MacArthur Blvd & Pacific Coast Highway)
  • Newport Beach (Follow 55 freeway south, and continue on down Newport Blvd to Balboa Penninsula)
  • Huntington Beach (Dining and Drinking)
  • Laguna Beach (Arts and Dining)
  • Orange County Fair
  • Orange County Great Park
