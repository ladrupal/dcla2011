Chris Charlton, Event Organizer

Chris helped grow the Drupal vibe in Los Angeles. Our meetups are also popular networking nights. Originating with less than 100 members, Chris has pushed membership above 600 and remains a Drupal junkie; speaking at many conferences and camps nearby and across the country. Through support and mentoring, our local Drupal group turned into a powerful group of trendsetters.

Chris is an author, Drupal contributor, and has been a leader over the years helping many Drupal events nationwide, and is proud to call Los Angeles his home.

Mike Stewart

Mike has been involved with the Drupal community by organizing meetups, sprints/lab sessions, and used to record the video archives of meetings. He is an open source fan and has promoted Drupal & other FOSS projects at various events such as SCaLE & LinuxWorld, and of course Drupalcon participation.

Mike has been involved with the local Drupal community since at least 2007 and runs a company that specializes building websites based on Drupal: MediaDoneRight.

John Romine

John is Director of Computing for the School of Engineering at UC Irvine. John’s been working with Drupal since 2007, and with PHP and MySQL for about 10 years. John develops and supports Drupal sites on-campus and freelance, and is also using Drupal to help develop business applications. John has been the venue host for DrupalCamp LA @ UCI for the last three years.

John’s an OC native, and participates in the LA Drupal, OC Drupal, and OC PHP groups.
