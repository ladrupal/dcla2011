LA Drupal's 5th Annual DrupalCamp: August 6-7th, 2011!

———— DRUPALCAMP LA: SIGNUP NOW! ————

Registration is now open for DrupalCamp LA, August 6-7, 2011!
This year we’re all indoors at the UC Irvine Conference Center,
with less blazing sun and more Drupal goodness.

…. RSVP by registering today!

Over two days this Summer fellow Drupal users come to share and learn
the next steps of Drupal. Learn more about Drupal 7. Get a hold on
Drupal production using modules, themes, tools, and anything else our
community has conjured up. People around you are registered for the camp,
what about you?!? *RSVP by registering today and pick the sessions
that interest you most. Remember, attending the camp is free but registering
with an RSVP is required.*

LOGIN NOW – http://2011.drupalcampla.com

/NOTE: If you registered last year then your account is waiting for you to
login and update your profile to pick what days you will be attending,
otherwise we have you marked as not attending./

…. Saturday Nighttime Event at the Anthill Pub

This year we’ve again reserved the Anthill Pub for a private party for DrupalCamp attendees, Saturday night from 7PM-Midnight. The Pub
will have their kitchen open for dinner so you will be able to purchase
something to eat while hanging out. You’ll want to bring cash; so take
note of the ATM machines next to the Pub.

…. Less sun, more fun

You can beat the heat this year, since we’re all indoors at the air
conditioned UC Irvine Conference Center. Bring cash for your lunch
and parking ($10/day). And, yes, there are ATMs on campus.

…. Stay on-campus and walk to camp from your room

If you are coming from out of town then stay overnight on-campus at
Hotel UCI like other fellow DrupalCampers, and walk from your room
to camp (and walk back after the pub party :-). Space is still available,
but they were sold out last year and will fill up soon. Pay by cash or
check when you arrive, or by credit card when you reserve (at least
10 days in advance). Get a two-bedroom apartment for 2 people for
$100/night, find a roommate and split the cost. Discounted
rates are also available at nearby hotels. For all the camp details
please visit: http://2011.drupalcampla.com/information

…. Volunteering

This is a community event. We need volunteers to help setup in the
morning, help through the day, and help with cleanup at the end. Its not
hard labor and its needed so please consider volunteering. Signup to volunteer now.

…. Presentations & Sessions

Last year we had over 50 sessions, and this year we have more space
than ever. Pick your own personalized schedule so we can place sessions
in the right sized rooms. http://2011.drupalcampla.com/sessions

We are still accepting new sessions. If you have a session idea,
add a session now. We ask that you hurry with your submissions
because people need to pick it in time for us to find the right
room for your session.

…. Are you presenting? Then get ready before the camp

We ask all those who plan to or already have submitted a session to please
read the info online about what we need you to install on your machines to
perfectly record your session to later share as a video with camp attendees
and Drupal community members worldwide.
http://2011.drupalcampla.com/information/presenters

…. Sponsors

We could not pull this event off without the support of the community and our
wonderful sponsors and we still need your help! Please consider sponsoring;
info available at: http://2011.drupalcampla.com/sponsorship
